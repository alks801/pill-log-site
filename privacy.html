Privacy Policy

PillLog

This Privacy Policy explains how PillLog handles your data. PillLog is designed to be privacy-first and does not require a user account.

Data Collection

PillLog does not collect, transmit, or store your personal medication data on external servers. Medication names, schedules, intake records, notes, reminders, and settings are stored locally on your device.

Personal Information

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, location, health profile, or device identifiers through the app.

Health Data

PillLog is a personal logging tool. Any medication-related information you enter stays on your device unless you choose to export or share it yourself.

Analytics & Tracking

PillLog does not use third-party analytics, advertising frameworks, or cross-app tracking. Your activity is not tracked across apps or websites.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, locally stored app data may be removed from your device according to iOS behavior.

Notifications

PillLog may use local notifications to remind you about scheduled medication intake. These notifications are created on your device and do not require sending your medication data to our servers.

Pro Features

If PillLog offers paid features, purchases may be processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive your full payment details.

Third-Party Services

The app does not share your medication data with third-party services.

Children’s Privacy

PillLog does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since the app does not collect personal data, no child data is stored or processed by us.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the app’s functionality changes. Any updates will be published on this page.
