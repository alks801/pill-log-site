Quick troubleshooting

  • Restart the app.
  • Restart your iPhone.
  • Make sure you are using the latest app version from the App Store.
  • Check that notifications are allowed in iOS Settings if reminders are not appearing.
  • If Face ID or Touch ID is unavailable, check that biometric authentication is enabled on your device.

FAQ

Is an account required?

No. PillLog works without sign-in.

Where is my data stored?

Your medication records are stored locally on your device.

Does PillLog provide medical advice?

No. PillLog is only a personal medication intake journal and reminder app. Always consult your doctor for medical decisions.

Why am I not receiving reminders?

Make sure notifications are enabled for PillLog in iOS Settings and that reminders are enabled for the medicine inside the app.
